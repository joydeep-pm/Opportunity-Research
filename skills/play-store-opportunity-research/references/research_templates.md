## Opportunity Scoring Rubric

Use weighted scoring (100-point scale):
- Demand proof (install/review evidence): 30
- Pain intensity (repeat complaints): 30
- Monetization clarity: 20
- Build complexity advantage: 20

Complexity scoring:
- Low complexity: 20
- Medium complexity: 12
- High complexity: 6
